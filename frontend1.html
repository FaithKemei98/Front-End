ABout Our Shop!


Sweet Cakes is a bakery specializing in fresh, quality cakes and pastries at reasonable prices. I come from a home setup where cooking and baking is a huge part of life. When I moved to Nairobi, I couldn’t find a cake that seemed fresh and homemade, so I decided to turn my passion for baking into a business. At sweet cakes, we are known for the uniqueness and large variety of our cakes – from forest cakes with fresh whipping cream and fruit to chocolate fudge cakes and banana cakes – and the creativity and customization that goes into them. Most importantly, we never compromise on quality; our cakes will always taste like home.
